RFQ extension for Magento community edition
Request for Quote was the requirement of one of the projects I did for my previous employer. Aim was to replace Add to Cart functionality with Request for Quote. It was done by adding RFQ button with Add to Cart button. User can add products to their RFQ list. Save it for later or Submit the RFQ. On submission the RFQ will remain open for a given amount of time for the vendors chosen by the customers. Vendors will provide their prices and any supporting documents as the reply to the RFQ with in the time. Customer choses the most suitable RFQ response and can add products to cart on vendor provided prices.
