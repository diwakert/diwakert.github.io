What projects exactly?

Some major projects:

Request for Quote Extension
It was the requirement of one of the projects I did for my previous employer. Aim was to replace Add to Cart functionality with Request for Quote. It was done by adding RFQ button with Add to Cart button. User can add products to their RFQ list. Save it for later or Submit the RFQ. On submission the RFQ will remain open for a given amount of time for the vendors chosen by the customers. Vendors will provide their prices and any supporting documents as the reply to the RFQ with in the time. Customer choses the most suitable RFQ response and can add products to cart on vendor provided prices.
Subscription Product Type Extension
Another project requirement working with my previous employer. Aim was to introduce a new product type Subscription. It was a complex product that can have other type of products as child products like Grouped or Configurable products. Product page of such product will show subscription terms and subscription items and a start date as a required field in add to cart form. An active subscription had renew or de-activate funcionality in back-end.
